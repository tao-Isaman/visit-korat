## About Dan Kwian Pottery Village

Dan Kwian is a charming village about 15 kilometers south of Korat city, renowned for its distinctive pottery tradition spanning over 300 years. The name "Dan Kwian" means "ox-cart checkpoint," referring to its historical role as a trading post.

The village's unique selling point is its special clay, which contains a high iron content that gives the finished pottery its characteristic rust-red and dark brown colors. The clay is found only in this area, making Dan Kwian pottery truly one-of-a-kind.

## What to See & Do

- **Pottery Workshops** — Watch artisans hand-craft pottery using traditional techniques
- **Try It Yourself** — Several shops offer pottery-making classes for visitors
- **Shopping** — Browse dozens of shops selling everything from decorative pieces to functional cookware
- **Dan Kwian Museum** — Learn about the history and techniques of local pottery

## Tips

- Great for buying unique souvenirs at factory prices
