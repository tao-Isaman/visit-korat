## About Save One Market

Save One is Korat's most famous night market and one of the largest in Isan. Spanning a huge area near the city center, this open-air market comes alive every evening with hundreds of vendors selling everything from street food to clothing, electronics, and handicrafts.

## What to Find

### Food Zone
The food section is legendary — rows upon rows of stalls serving:
- Pad Mee Korat (Korat-style stir-fried noodles)
- Grilled meats and Isan sausages (sai krok Isan)
- Som Tam (papaya salad) in every style imaginable
- Fresh seafood BBQ
- Desserts and Thai sweets
- Fresh fruit smoothies

### Shopping Zone
- Clothing and accessories at bargain prices
- Local handicrafts and Dan Kwian pottery
- Electronics and gadgets
- Home decor items

### Entertainment
- Live music stages
- Carnival games and rides
- Themed photo spots

## Tips

- Come hungry — the food is the main attraction
- Bring cash (most vendors don't accept cards)
- Arrive early (around 17:00) to beat the crowds
- Closed on Monday and Tuesday
