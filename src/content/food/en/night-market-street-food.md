## Korat Night Market Street Food Guide

Korat's night markets offer some of the best street food experiences in Isan. Here's what to look for:

## Must-Try Street Foods

### Savory
- **Moo Ping** (หมูปิ้ง) — Grilled marinated pork skewers, 10 THB each
- **Look Chin Ping** (ลูกชิ้นปิ้ง) — Grilled meatball skewers with spicy sauce
- **Khao Jee** (ข้าวจี่) — Grilled sticky rice with egg, a classic Isan snack
- **Sai Krok Isan** (ไส้กรอกอีสาน) — Fermented sausages served with ginger and chili
- **Fried Insects** — Crickets, silkworms, and more for the adventurous!

### Sweet
- **Khanom Buang** (ขนมเบื้อง) — Crispy Thai crepes with sweet or savory toppings
- **Roti** — Thai-style flatbread with banana, egg, and condensed milk
- **Mango Sticky Rice** — Sweet coconut sticky rice with fresh mango (seasonal)
- **Kanom Krok** — Coconut pudding pancakes

### Drinks
- **Cha Yen** — Thai iced tea
- **Nam Manao** — Fresh lime juice
- **Fresh coconut water** — Straight from the shell
- **Smoothies** — Fresh tropical fruit blended with ice

## Top Night Markets

1. **Save One** — The biggest and most famous
2. **Manat Night Market** — Local favorite, less touristy
3. **Walking Street (Thanon Khon Dern)** — Weekend pedestrian market
