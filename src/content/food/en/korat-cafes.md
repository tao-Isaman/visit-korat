## Korat's Cafe Culture

Korat has seen a cafe explosion in recent years. From converted warehouses to garden cafes surrounded by nature, the city offers a diverse coffee scene that rivals Bangkok.

## Types of Cafes

### Garden & Nature Cafes
Many cafes in the Korat countryside offer stunning garden settings with lush greenery. Perfect for escaping the city heat.

### Modern & Minimalist
The city center has sleek, Instagram-worthy cafes with specialty coffee and creative drinks.

### Heritage & Vintage
Converted shophouses in the old city area serve coffee in charming retro settings.

## What to Order

- **Oliang** — Traditional Thai-style black coffee
- **Specialty single-origin** — Many cafes source beans from northern Thailand
- **Iced cocoa** — A local favorite for beating the heat
- **Homemade pastries** — Each cafe has its own signature bakes

## Tips

- Most cafes are air-conditioned — perfect for afternoon breaks
- Prices range from 60-150 THB per drink
- Many cafes near Khao Yai offer mountain views
