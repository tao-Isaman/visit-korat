## About the Fair

The annual Dan Kwian Pottery Fair celebrates the 300-year-old pottery tradition of this famous village. Master potters demonstrate their techniques while visitors can try their hand at the craft.

## Highlights

- Live pottery demonstrations by master artisans
- Hands-on pottery workshops
- Special limited-edition pieces for sale
- Cultural performances
- Local food stalls
- Factory prices on pottery
