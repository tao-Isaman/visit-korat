## About the Festival

The Thao Suranari Festival is the most important cultural event in Korat, held annually from March 23 to April 3. It honors Khunying Mo (Lady Mo), the heroine who saved the city from Lao invaders in 1826.

## Highlights

- Grand opening ceremony with city officials and traditional rituals
- Colorful parades through the city streets
- Traditional Thai dance and music performances
- Light and sound shows at the monument
- Local food fair with Korat specialties
- Craft markets featuring Dan Kwian pottery
- Beauty pageants and cultural competitions
