## About the Festival

The annual Phimai Festival transforms the ancient Khmer ruins into a spectacular venue for cultural celebration. Held every November, it draws visitors from across Thailand.

## Highlights

- Dramatic sound and light show projected onto the ancient ruins
- Traditional long-boat racing on the Moon River
- Khmer-inspired dance performances
- Historical reenactments
- Local food and craft markets
- Marathon and cycling events
