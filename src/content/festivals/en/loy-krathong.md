## About Loy Krathong in Korat

Loy Krathong is celebrated nationwide, but Korat's celebrations along the Lam Takhong River are especially beautiful. Locals and visitors gather to float decorated krathong (lotus-shaped vessels) to pay respect to the water spirits.

## Highlights

- Floating krathong on Lam Takhong River
- Beauty pageants (Nang Noppamas)
- Fireworks displays
- Cultural performances
- Food markets along the river
