## About Songkran in Korat

Songkran (Thai New Year) in Korat is renowned as one of the most energetic celebrations in all of Thailand. The city streets transform into massive water battlegrounds while traditional ceremonies honor the past.

## Highlights

- Massive water fights throughout the city center
- Traditional merit-making ceremonies at temples
- Bathing of Buddha images
- Paying respects to elders with scented water
- Parades and beauty pageants
- Live music and entertainment stages
- Street food festivals
