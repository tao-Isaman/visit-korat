## Day 1: Korat City — History & Culture

### Morning
- Arrive in Korat and check into your hotel
- Visit **Thao Suranari Monument** and the old city area
- Explore **Chumphon Gate** and the remnants of the old city wall

### Lunch
- Try **Pad Mee Korat** at a local restaurant near the monument

### Afternoon
- Visit **Wat Sala Loi** — the unique boat-shaped temple
- Explore **Wat Phra Narai Maharat**
- Browse shops around the city center

### Evening
- Head to **Save One Night Market** for dinner and shopping
- Try Moo Ping, Som Tam, and Sai Krok Isan

## Day 2: Phimai Day Trip — Ancient Khmer Heritage

### Morning
- Take an early bus to Phimai (about 1.5 hours)
- Explore **Phimai Historical Park** — spend 2-3 hours

### Lunch
- Eat at a local noodle shop near the temple

### Afternoon
- Visit **Phimai National Museum**
- See **Sai Ngam** — the enormous banyan tree grove
- Return to Korat city

### Evening
- Dinner at a local Isan restaurant
- Walk around the illuminated monument area

## Day 3: Khao Yai — Nature & Adventure

### Morning
- Drive or take a bus to Khao Yai area (1.5 hours)
- Enter **Khao Yai National Park** early
- Hike to **Haew Suwat Waterfall**

### Midday
- Pack a picnic or eat at park facilities
- Drive to viewpoints and look for wildlife

### Afternoon
- Visit **PB Valley Winery** for a tasting tour
- Stop at a scenic cafe in the Khao Yai area

### Evening
- Return to Korat or head back to Bangkok
- Optional: Night safari tour in the park (book in advance!)
