## Khao Yai Area

The Khao Yai area, centered around Pak Chong district, is Korat province's premier nature destination. About 1.5 hours northwest of Korat city, this mountainous region offers a cool escape with stunning scenery.

## Attractions

### Nature
- **Khao Yai National Park** — Thailand's oldest and most visited national park
- **Waterfalls** — Haew Narok, Haew Suwat, and dozens more
- **Wildlife watching** — Wild elephants, gibbons, hornbills

### Wine & Farms
- **PB Valley Khao Yai Winery** — Thailand's largest winery with tours and tastings
- **GranMonte Vineyard** — Boutique winery with excellent wines
- **Jim Thompson Farm** — Beautiful farm open seasonally (Dec-Jan)
- **Primo Piazza** — Italian-themed village, popular for photos

### Activities
- **Hiking trails** in and around the national park
- **Night safari** tours to spot wildlife
- **ATV riding** and zip-lining at adventure parks
- **Stargazing** — Minimal light pollution makes for stunning night skies

## Where to Stay

The Khao Yai area is famous for boutique resorts and farm stays, many with stunning mountain views. Book ahead on weekends and holidays.

## Getting There

About 130 km from Korat city via Highway 2 (Mittraphap). Buses run regularly from Korat Bus Terminal 2 to Pak Chong.
