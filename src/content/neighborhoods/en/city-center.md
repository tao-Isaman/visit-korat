## City Center & Old Town

The city center of Korat is where history meets modern life. Centered around the iconic Thao Suranari Monument and the ancient Chumphon Gate, this area is the cultural heart of the city.

## What to See

### Historical Sites
- **Thao Suranari Monument** — Start here to orient yourself
- **Chumphon Gate** — The western gate of the old city wall, beautifully restored
- **Wat Phra Narai Maharat** — Temple with ancient Khmer influences
- **Wat Sala Loi** — The famous boat-shaped temple

### Markets & Shopping
- **Save One Night Market** — Korat's biggest night market
- **The Mall Korat** — Major shopping center
- **Klang Plaza** — Shopping and dining complex

### Food
The city center is packed with food options, from street vendors around the monument to restaurants on Jomsurangyat Road.

## Getting Around

The city center is walkable for most attractions. Songthaew (shared pickup trucks) run fixed routes through the area. Tuk-tuks and motorcycle taxis are available for longer distances.

## Tips

- The area around the monument is especially lively in the evening
- Many ATMs and exchange services available
- Hotels and guesthouses range from budget to mid-range
