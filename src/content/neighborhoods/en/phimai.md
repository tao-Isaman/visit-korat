## Phimai District

Located about 60 km northeast of Korat city, the small town of Phimai punches well above its weight in terms of historical significance. It was once an important city in the Khmer Empire, connected directly to Angkor by an ancient highway.

## Main Attractions

- **Phimai Historical Park** — The star attraction, a stunning Khmer temple complex
- **Sai Ngam** — An enormous banyan tree grove, one of the largest in Thailand
- **Phimai National Museum** — Excellent collection of Khmer artifacts
- **Old Town** — Quiet streets with traces of the ancient city layout

## Food in Phimai

Phimai has its own food scene:
- Local noodle shops around the temple area
- Fresh market near the bus station
- Rice-based dishes and Isan cuisine

## Getting There

- **By bus:** Regular buses from Korat Bus Terminal 2 (about 1.5 hours)
- **By car:** Highway 2 north, then Route 206 east
- **By tour:** Many tour operators in Korat offer Phimai day trips

## Tips

- Can be visited as a day trip from Korat city
- The annual Phimai Festival (November) features boat races and temple illuminations
- Combine with a visit to Phanomwan Khmer ruins on the way
